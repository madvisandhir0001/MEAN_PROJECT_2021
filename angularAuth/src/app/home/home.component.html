WHO WE ARE

Our experienced team can help design and manage the creation of beautiful and practical spaces within a range of different properties and styles. Our mission is to provide luxury home staging and interior design services to clients across a range of price points from first time home buyers living in properties as small as a studio to those looking to sell their luxury penthouse or town home.

Partner with us and we will be available to you on the phone, at the office and through emails throughout the duration of the project.

We value the trust you place in us and constantly strive to be worthy of it.

SERVICES

Our services include end-to-end support, from getting statutory approvals, tenders, to design and construction. We offer a team of architects, engineers, interior designers, value’s as well as a well-spread network of artisans and allied construction services. We also specialize in furnishing the buildings and doing custom work with our network of national and international partners.


INTERIOR DESIGN

Practical and holistic approach to design and renovation. Advising on styles, options, feasibility & cost. Schemes to suit your lifestyle and sourcing fittings, furnishings and suggesting colors, lighting and much more!

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Preparing schedules of work, finishes & timelines. Finding suitable contractors & specialists including arranging quotes. Acting as interface between clients and specialists, Problem-solving and troubleshooting.

HOME STAGING

Prepare your property so it sells quickly and for the optimal market price. Arrange quick execution of the project including decoration, repairs and furnishing. Ensure the property appeals to the maximum number of viewers.

ARCHITECTURE DESIGN

Our architecture team is always striving to think out-of-the-box, following a very intimate process of discovering each client’s personal style. We believe good design is produced from careful study and research.

DESIGN + BUILD

Our design team can take your vision from start to construction. Once the design is complete we start construction. At this point we review any final changes you might want to make, finalize contracts, start date, and final schedule
